Committee Vision

The discussion began with the static portion of the website. Once the Committee had a clear understanding of the content to be included, it moved on to the second section—Questionnaire & Feedback Management. Broadly, the Committee envisions two main components:

  • The static portion of the website
  • The dynamic portion of the website (Questionnaire & Feedback Management)

Static Portion Components

The Committee wants the following components in the static portion of the website with a simple, clear, and intuitive UI:

  1. Get the Website Up & Running with Some Read-outs
    • Information about the Formation of the current Union-State Relations Committee
    • Assembly Announcement of the current Committee (Video & Text)
    • Press release on the Formation of the Committee (English & Tamil)
  2. Photographs and brief Bios of the Committee Chairman & 2 other Committee members
  3. Information about the Formation of past Union-State Relations Committees
  4. Rajamannar Committee
    • Terms of Reference (ToR)
    • Questionnaire for the public (Stakeholders)
    • Link to Report of the Rajamannar Committee
  5. Sarkaria Committee
    • Terms of Reference (ToR)
    • Questionnaire for the public (Stakeholders)
    • Link to Report of the Sarkaria Committee
  6. Punchi Committee
    • Terms of Reference (ToR)
    • Questionnaire for the public (Stakeholders)
    • Link to Report of the Sarkaria Committee
  7. Announcements made by Ex Tamil Nadu Chief Ministers
    • Thiru. C.N. Annadurai’s Announcements
    • Thiru. M. Karunanidhi’s Announcements
  8. Links to Historic Announcements
  9. Re-direction Link to the India Code Website (Constitution of India)
  10. Re-direction Link for the Constituent Assembly Debates
  11. Famous Quotes on Federalism
    • Thiru. C.N. Annadurai’s Quotes
    • Thiru. M. Karunanidhi’s Quotes
    • Notable Quotes from the Constituent Assembly Debates (e.g. K. Santhanam)
