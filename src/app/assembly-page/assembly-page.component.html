Announcement Overview

The Assembly of Tamil Nadu officially announced the formation of the Union-State Relations Committee. The goal of the committee is to re-examine the distribution of powers between the Union and State governments, ensuring a more inclusive and decentralized framework.

The announcement reflects a renewed commitment to federal cooperation and public involvement.
